Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This policy explains exactly what information the My Library Desk app collects and how it is used.

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Library Desk mobile application ("App") developed for library management. By registering or using the App, the library administrator and its members agree to the collection and use of information as described below. We only collect data that is strictly required to operate and manage the library.

2 How We Use Your Information

All collected data is used exclusively for library management purposes. We use it to:

  • Register and manage the library, its administrator account, and settings
  • Add, update, and manage member profiles with identity verification
  • Create and configure library shifts with pricing and timings
  • Assign seats and lockers to members and track availability
  • Record, display, and manage payment and subscription transactions
  • Track member active/inactive status and enrollment history
  • Generate internal reports for the library administrator
  • Send payment due reminders and membership expiry alerts

3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard security practices to protect all information from unauthorized access or misuse.

🔒 Encrypted Passwords
🛡️ Secure API (HTTPS)
🔑 Token-Based Auth
📦 Server-Side Validation
All member data, payment records, seat and locker assignments are stored only as long as your library account is active. Data can be permanently deleted upon request by the library administrator.

4 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes. Data may only be accessed in these limited cases:

  • By the authorized library administrator through the App
  • When required by applicable law or a valid court order
  • To prevent fraud or protect the security of the App

5 Your Rights

As a member or library administrator, you have the following rights over your data:

📋 View your data
✏️ Update information
🗑️ Request deletion
📤 Export records
🚫 Withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact the library administrator or reach us directly via the contact details below.

6 Children's Privacy

My Library Desk is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child's data has been submitted, please contact us immediately and we will remove it promptly.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
